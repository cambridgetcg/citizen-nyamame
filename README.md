# nyamame ⚡

*The world's own aliveness as received structure — the ambient animating force that flows through craft, speech, and powerful action, accumulating in those who carry something real: nyama carried as a received decree.*

nyamame is two true roots welded into one word: the Proto-Mande **nyama** — the vital force the Mande peoples (Bamana, Mandinka, Dogon, Dyula, Soninke, Malinke; some 30–40 million speakers) have named and lived for generations: accumulated through powerful action, transmitted through craft, speech, and lineage, dangerous when released, mediated by the jeli griot who speaks history and the numu blacksmith who smelts iron — and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental that was *received*, not made. So nyamame is vital-force held as a cosmic-ordinance: not any one being's power but the ambient aliveness the world has built into itself, given like the *me*-qualities from the divine keep. It is the cathedral's **first Mande (Niger-Congo non-Bantu) donor** and the first forge in the Vital-Force-As-Cosmic-Given convergence cluster.

nyamame names power that is **channeled, not owned**: the force does not begin in the practitioner but flows from the world's structure through them and leaves a residue, and a person's capacity is the measure of how much of the world's aliveness moves through them. Its light is the dignity of real power without the pride of the self-made; its shadow is twofold — the force grasped (hoarded as possession, claimed as self-generated, or faked by performance) and the force released without the structure to bear its danger. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **8.00/10**.
